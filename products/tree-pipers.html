Free delivery and our 30 days, no questions asked return policy. Lightly worn shoes get donated to Soles4Souls..

Searching for machine-washable sneakers that won’t lose their shape? All our shoes can go right into the wash. To prep, pull out the laces and insoles and place your shoes in a delicates bag (pro tip: a pillowcase works too) and toss them in the washing machines. Choose a gentle cycle with cold water and mild detergent. Next, steer clear of the dryer, and instead shake out any excess water and set aside to air dry. Shoes need a little extra TLC? Swap in some brand new laces and insoles.
